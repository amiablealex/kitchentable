Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Welcome to Kitchen Table

Kitchen Table is a private app for staying connected with family and friends. We take your privacy seriously and keep things simple.

What Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Your username, email address, and display name
  • Your Responses: The answers you share to daily questions
  • Usage Information: When you last logged in

Important: Your password is encrypted using bcrypt hashing. We cannot see your actual password - not even the app administrator.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Provide you access to your Kitchen Table
  • Show your responses to other members of your table
  • Send password reset emails if you forget your password

We never sell your data. We never share it with advertisers. This is a private app for staying connected.

Who Can See Your Information

  • Your Table Members: People in your Kitchen Table can see your username, display name, and responses to questions
  • Only You: Your email address is private
  • Nobody: Your password is encrypted and cannot be seen by anyone, including us

Cookies

We use a single cookie to keep you logged in. That's it. No tracking, no analytics, no advertising cookies.

Your Rights

You can:

  • View your data: Everything you've shared is visible in the app
  • Change your information: Update your display name in Settings
  • Delete your account: Permanently delete your account and all data in Settings
  • Request your data: Contact us and we'll provide all your information

Data Security

We protect your information using:

  • Encrypted password storage (bcrypt hashing)
  • Secure authentication tokens
  • Regular database backups

Children's Privacy

Kitchen Table is intended for users 13 years and older. If you're under 13, please get a parent's permission before using the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top if we do.

Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Need to request your data or delete your account?

We're here to help - just reach out through your usual contact method.
